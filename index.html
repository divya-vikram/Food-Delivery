Gourmet Burger

Sink your teeth into our Classic Gourmet Burger, crafted with a juicy beef patty, melted cheddar cheese, crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and tangy pickles, all nestled between a toasted brioche bun.Accompanied by our signature smoky aioli sauce, this burger promises a burst of flavor in every bite.

Margherita Pizza

Enjoy a slice of Italy with our Authentic Margherita Pizza. Made with a thin, crispy crust, topped with fresh basil leaves, rich tomato sauce, and creamy mozzarella cheese, this pizza is a delightful symphony of traditional Italian flavors. Perfect for a quick lunch or a cozy dinner.

Chicken Caesar Salad

Delight in our Classic Chicken Caesar Salad, featuring tender grilled chicken breast, crisp Romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and freshly shaved Parmesan cheese. Tossed in a creamy Caesar dressing, this salad is both healthy and satisfying, ideal for a light yet hearty meal.

Experience the vibrant flavors of our Vegan Buddha Bowl, brimming with nutritious goodness. This bowl includes quinoa, roasted sweet potatoes, avocado slices, chickpeas, and fresh greens, all drizzled with a tangy tahini dressing. Perfect for those seeking a wholesome and delicious vegan option.
